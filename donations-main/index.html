Welcome to HOPE! Hope Inc is a NGO that has been a forerunner for humanitarian services nationwide with over 25 years of operation. We have brought smiles to million of faces through our services and we hope to continue doing just that.
What do we do

We provide financial support for students by providing them with paid flexible part time jobs. We have been under-staffed since the covid-pandemic and the workload is greater than the employees available; this has hugely reduced the percentage of people that are accessible to our services. Therefore, we have outsourced some jobs to college students and staff, with a weekly pay of $250-300.

U.S. Individuals response

With over 100K families already funded with our donations, join the movement to help get donations and cash relief to U.S. households impacted by COVID-19. Your job will entail disbursing donations to shelters, orphages, the homeless, individuals in needs, local clinics, and others who need our help and support.
